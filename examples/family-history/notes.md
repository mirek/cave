# Notes from Grandma Maria's 90th birthday

Talked family history with Grandma Maria today. Her father Jan was born in
Kraków — she says 1932, but her cousin has always insisted it was 1931.
Jan's mother Helena ran a bakery on Long Street until the war. Family lore
says Helena's father — my great-great-grandfather — fought in the 1920 war;
Maria is only fairly sure it's true (60%, say), nobody has papers.

Maria's daughter is my mum, Anna. Oh, and last spring's DNA test finally
settled it: the "cousin Piotr" branch really is related — 88% match.
